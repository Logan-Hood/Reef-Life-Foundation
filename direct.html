Reef Life Foundation believes all ocean animals deserve a home. Globally, coral reefs are dying at unprecedented rates, leaving marine species and coastal communities without food or storm protection. To combat this, we developed an unprecedented coral reef restoration system called IntelliReefs that fosters new coral growth, increases fish abundance & enhances biodiversity.

Every donation directly supports our campaign, "Building Homes for the Ocean's Homeless" which involves the restoration of degraded coral reefs & kelp forests.
