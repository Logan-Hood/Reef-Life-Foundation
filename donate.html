How to Donate

Gift a Reef

Looking for an eco-friendly gift? "Gift a Reef" turns your donation into living coral reefs and kelp forests in areas that need them most. Each certificate spreads ocean awareness, perfect for friends, family, or in memory of loved ones.

Ocean Gifts

Explore our ocean-inspired jewelry and gifts that support coral reef conservation. Each purchase aids restoration efforts. Find something special for yourself or others while making a meaningful impact.

Direct Donation

Reef Life Foundation works to provide homes for all sea creatures. By donating to our "Building Homes for the Ocean's Homeless" campaign, you help us restore damaged coral reefs and kelp forests, creating vital habitats for marine life.
