Building Homes For The Ocean's Homeless

Your Impact
Our Mission
Donate

Reef Life Foundation helps build new homes for sea creatures. We use a special material called Oceanite that looks and feels like real coral reefs. We work with local people to plant more coral and help fish find places to live. Our goal is to make the ocean healthier for both sea animals and people.
